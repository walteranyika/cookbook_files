Recipe of Moong Dal Chila

Ingredients

  1. For Moong Dal Cheela
  2. 2 cups split moong dal (without skin)
  3. 1 green chilli, finely chopped (optional)
  4. 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves, finely chopped
  5. Salt to taste
  6. 2 tbsp Olive oil/ghee
  7. For Topping Option 1:
  8. 100 gm mushrooms, sliced
  9. 1 tsp oil
  10. 100 gm paneer
  11. Salt to taste
  12. Freshly crushed black peppercorns
  13. For Topping Option 2:
  14. 200 gm tofu
  15. 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  16. 1 green chilli, finely chopped
  17. Salt to taste
  18. 1-2 pinch turmeric powder
  19. 1 tsp oil

How To Cook

  1. Wash the moong dal under running water, till water comes out clean. Then cover it with water and leave it to soak for 2 hours
  2. Grind the moong dal in a food processor to make a fine paste. Add more water if required to get the consistency of crepe batter.
  3. Heat a griddle or cast iron skillet on medium heat. Add oil or ghee and then swirl to coat all sides of the pan.
  4. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the pan and with the help of a ladle spread the it into a circular shape from the center outwards.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minute till its underside is browned. Then flip it and cook till the other side is golden too.
  6. Remove from pan and serve immediately with topping of your choice.
  7. For topping option 1:
  8. Heat 1 tsp oil in a pan, then add sliced paneer and cook from both sides. Remove from pan and keep it aside.
  9. Now add sliced mushrooms, cook for 5-6 minutes till mushrooms start to caramelize and add salt and crushed black peppers.
  10. Remove from heat and keep aside.
  11. For topping option 2:
  12. Heat oil in another pan, add chopped onion and chilies. Cook for 2-3 minutes so that the onions are cooked but not browned.
  13. Add turmeric powder and salt followed by crumbled tofu. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  14. Serve hot!
