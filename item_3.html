Recipe of Chicken 65, Dakshin

Ingredients

  1. 20 pieces of chicken, around 12 gms each
  2. 1 Tbsp chili powder   
  3. 1 tsp turmeric powder    
  4. 1 Tbsp ginger garlic paste    
  5. 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  6. 2 lemons, juice
  7. 1 Tbsp rice powder    
  8. 1 Tbsp corn flour    
  9. 1 egg, beaten   
  10. Salt to taste 
  11. Oil for frying
  12. For the garam masala:
  13. 1 marathi mokku or caper
  14. 3 star anise
  15. 2 mace flower
  16. 1/2 nutmeg, the size of a peanut
  17. 4 cloves
  18. 1 cinnamon sticks (2 inch)
  19. 1/2 tsp raw rice, roasted

How To Cook

  1. Mix all the ingredients namely chili powder, turmeric, ginger garlic
  2. paste, lemon juice and beaten egg with rice flour & corn flour.
  3. This will give you your marinade.
  4. Smear the marination on the chicken pieces and leave it for about 30 minutes.
  5. Fry the chicken in hot oil.
  6. Serve with raw onion and lemon wedges.
