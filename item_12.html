Recipe of Dal Makhni (My Yellow Table)

Ingredients

  1. 250 gm maa ki dal
  2. 1 tsp red chilli powder
  3. 250 ml tomato puree
  4. 500 gms butter
  5. 1 Tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1 tsp kasuri methi powder
  7. 3 Tbsp cream
  8. Salt

How To Cook

  1. Rinse maa ki dal and boil till soft & the skin falls off.
  2. Add salt, red chilli powder, tomato puree & butter.
  3. Allow to cook. Add kasuri methi powder & cream.
  4. Stir well & serve hot.
