Recipe of Mango Rice

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup of cooked rice
  2. 1/2 cup of raw mango (grated)
  3. 1/2tsp of mustard
  4. 1/2 tsp of urad dal
  5. 1/2 tsp of channa dal
  6. 1 tsp of groundnut
  7. 2 green chilli
  8. 1 curry leaves
  9. 1/4 tsp of sprig turmeric powder
  10. 3 tsp of sesame oil
  11. Salt to taste

How To Cook

  1. In a pan add sesame oil and mustard. Once mustard crackles add urad dal, channa dal and green chilli. Now add curry leaves, asafoetida and turmeric powder.
  2. Grate raw mango and keep ready.
  3. Cook and cool the rice completely.
  4. Add the tempering and grated mangoÂ  with salt to taste.
  5. Mix well.
  6. Mango Rice is ready. Serve with any chips.
